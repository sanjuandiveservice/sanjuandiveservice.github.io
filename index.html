Pricing

We believe in fully transparent and affordable pricing, with no hidden fees for setup/consumables.

Base rates

  • Hourly rate $100 (1 hour minimum, then billed to the next half hour — $50/30 mins)
    Note: Total job time (Arrival–Departure), not dive time.
  • Hull cleaning labor $4.50/foot
    No hourly minimum if hull cleaning charges are applied.
  • Zinc replacement labor $20
  • Same-day emergency charge $50
  • Excessive growth charge $2.50/foot
    (customer will be notified before work is performed)
  • Ferry-serviced island travel charge $60
  • Non-ferry-serviced island: variable travel charge — call for information
